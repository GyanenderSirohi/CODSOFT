About Me

In the business since 2020

I am Gyanender Sirohi, skilled across various disciplines rather than specializing in just one, often achieving proficiency that surpasses that of a single master. My journey into knife making began in 2018, and by 2020, I had become adept in this craft, mastering the art of balancing strength and aesthetic appeal in each knife.

Over the years, I have extensively experimented with different materials for both knife blades and handles. The sheath, an integral component of any knife, is crafted uniquely by me using PVC, ensuring each one is distinctively designed to protect the blade effectively.

Additionally, I specialize in personalizing and customizing knives to meet individual preferences and requirements.
